#  Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s

- ✔ Think before posting and ensure your content is appropriate
- ✔ Maintain privacy settings to protect personal information
- ✔ Be respectful and polite in online interactions
- ✔ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
- ✔ Use social media for learning, networking, and positive engagement

## ❌ Don’ts

- ❌ Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- ❌ Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or abusive content
- ❌ Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling
- ❌ Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown sources
- ❌ Do not overshare or post content that can harm your reputation
