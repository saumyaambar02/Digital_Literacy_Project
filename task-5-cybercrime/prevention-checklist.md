# 🔴 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

---

## ✅ General Safety Tips

- Do not click unknown links  
  Always check the sender before clicking any link in emails or messages.

- Use strong passwords  
  Keep different passwords for different accounts and avoid simple passwords.

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
  Add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

- Do not share personal information  
  Never share passwords, OTPs, or personal details online.

- Verify before trusting  
  If a message looks urgent or too good to be true, check it before taking action.

- Keep apps and devices updated  
  Updates help protect your device from security threats.

---

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP  
  No bank or official will ever ask for it.

- Do not accept unknown payment requests  
  Receiving a request does not mean you will get money — you may lose it.

- Check before scanning QR codes  
  Only scan QR codes from trusted sources.

---

## 📢 Reporting Cyber Crime

- Report immediately if something goes wrong  

  - National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
  - Helpline Number: 1930
